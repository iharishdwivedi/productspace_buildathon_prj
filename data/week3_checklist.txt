WEEK3 CHECKLIST

Goal: Transition from guided learning to independent contribution.
Task Ownership: Aim to take ownership of a standard, medium-sized task from the backlog.
Code/Work Submission: Independently submit your first significant piece of work (e.g., a pull request, a design draft, an analysis report).
Incorporate Feedback: Actively seek and incorporate feedback from your team's review process.
Team Ceremonies: Move from "observer" to "participant" in sprint planning or team meetings, offering estimates or opinions.
Daily Stand-ups: Provide clear updates on your progress, plans, and any blockers.
Manager 1:1: Discuss your 30/60/90-day goals and get your manager's sign-off.
Project Roadmap: Review the product/project roadmap for the next quarter.
Coffee Chats (Leadership): Schedule a coffee chat with your manager's manager or a team lead.
Documentation: Contribute to the team wiki (Confluence); update any outdated documentation you find.
Tool Mastery: Demonstrate proficiency in the primary tools (Jira, GitHub, Figma, etc.).
Customer Focus: Sit in on a customer support call or review recent customer feedback (Zendesk tickets).
Role-Specific Guide: Re-review your specific guide (engineer_guide.txt, designer_guide.txt, analyst_guide.txt) for best practices.
Communication: Practice using the correct channel (Slack vs. Email vs. Jira) as per the communication_rules.txt.
Networking: Identify 1-2 Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) in other departments related to your work.
Company-Wide Meeting: Attend your first company-wide "All Hands" or "Town Hall" meeting.
Performance Feedback: Ask your manager, "How am I pacing against your expectations for my first 30 days?"
Onboarding Buddy: Schedule a check-out with your onboarding buddy to review Week 1-3.
Team Knowledge: Understand your team's key metrics (KPIs) or objectives (OKRs).
First Paycheck: Log in to the payroll system (Workday) and verify your first paycheck details.
Home Office: Confirm all your home office equipment has arrived and is set up correctly.
Benefits: Double-check that your insurance cards (digital or physical) have been received.
Learning Portal: Set a goal to complete one certification path or course relevant to your role.
Social: Attend a virtual team social event or an in-office team lunch.
Team Processes: Be able to explain the team's workflow (e.g., "code to production") to someone else.
Blockers: Practice identifying and clearly articulating any blockers you are facing.
Submit Timesheet: Complete and submit your timesheet for Week 3.
Review Company Values: Re-read the company_values.txt and find an example of one in action.
Org Structure: Understand who the key leaders are in your department's "skip-level" structure.
Look Ahead: Start looking at the backlog for potential tasks for the next sprint (Week 4).
