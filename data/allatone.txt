=== LEAVE POLICY ===
• Leave Year: Calendar year (Jan 1 - Dec 31)
• Privilege Leave (PL): 1.5 days accrued per month
• Casual Leave (CL): 8 days annual quota for personal matters
• Sick Leave (SL): 10 days annual quota for medical recovery
• Probation: No PL during first 3 months, CL/SL available pro-rata
• Carry Forward: Up to 15 days PL to next year, CL/SL lapse
• Encashment: Only PL exceeding 30 days upon resignation
• Application: All leaves via HRMS portal
• Notice Period: 2 weeks for PL >3 days, same-day notification for sick leave by 9:30 AM
• Medical Certificate: Required for SL >3 consecutive days
• Maternity: 26 weeks paid leave, Paternity: 10 days within 3 months of birth
• Bereavement: 3 days paid for immediate family death
• Comp-Off: Weekend/holiday work with manager approval, use within 30 days
• Sandwich Rule: Weekends between leave days count as leave
• Short Leave: 2 hours twice monthly without deduction
• Half-Day: 0.5 day increments (morning/afternoon)
• Loss of Pay: Salary deduction for exceeding leave balance
• Unauthorized Absence: >3 days triggers disciplinary action

=== SECURITY GUIDELINES ===
• Password Policy: Minimum 14 characters with complexity requirements
• Password Manager: Use company-provided 1Password for all credentials
• MFA: Mandatory on all supporting systems (SSO, email, code repos)
• Phishing: Report suspicious emails via "Report Phishing" button
• Data Classification: Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted levels
• Data Handling: Never email Confidential/Restricted to personal accounts
• Software: Only install authorized, licensed software via IT portal
• Public Wi-Fi: Avoid or use VPN when necessary
• VPN: Always connected when working remotely
• Physical Security: Wear ID badge, don't tailgate, escort visitors
• Clean Desk: Lock screen when away (Win+L / Cmd+Ctrl+Q)
• Device Security: Never leave laptops unattended in public
• Incident Reporting: Report loss/theft within 1 hour to IT
• USB Restrictions: Don't use unknown removable media
• Social Engineering: Verify identity of callers requesting information
• Annual Training: Mandatory security awareness training required

=== WEEKLY CHECKLISTS ===

Week 1 Focus: Setup and Initial Integration
• Day 1: IT setup (laptop, MFA, VPN), system logins, team introductions
• Day 1: Review contacts, company values, org structure, email signature setup
• Day 2: Schedule team coffee chats, first 1:1 with manager, review policies
• Day 3: Benefits enrollment, acknowledge conduct/security policies, join Slack channels
• Day 4: Complete compliance training, tool access, attend New Hire Orientation
• Day 5: Set 30/60/90-day goals, understand project board, first task assignment

Week 2 Focus: Learning and Integration
• Active participation in daily stand-ups and team meetings
• Review 3-6 months of project documentation in Confluence
• Shadow teammate, review their work (code/design/reports)
• Complete 1-2 small tasks, understand "Definition of Done"
• Cross-functional coffee chats outside immediate team
• Product demo attendance, architecture/design system review
• Customer persona understanding, competitor analysis
• Benefits and payroll confirmation, advanced tool exploration

Week 3 Focus: Independent Contribution
• Take ownership of medium-sized task from backlog
• Submit first significant work (PR, design draft, analysis)
• Move from observer to participant in team ceremonies
• Contribute to team wiki, update outdated documentation
• Coffee chat with skip-level manager or team lead
• Customer support call observation, feedback review
• Practice proper communication channels per guidelines
• Performance feedback request from manager

Week 4 Focus: Autonomy and 30-Day Review
• Independent task completion from sprint backlog
• Participate in task estimation and sprint planning
• Review teammate's work (code review, design review)
• Contribute to sprint retrospective meeting
• 30-Day review preparation with accomplishments list
• Complete New Hire survey, buddy program wrap-up
• Cross-functional impact understanding
• Tool customization for productivity, team contribution
