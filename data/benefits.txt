EMPLOYEE BENEFITS AND PERKS

1. Policy Scope: Outlines all non-salary compensation and perks available to full-time employees.
2. Health Insurance (Medical): Comprehensive Group Medical Coverage (GMC) for employee, spouse, and up to 2 children.
3. Parental Coverage: Option to add parents to the medical plan at a subsidized co-payment.
4. Insurance Sum Insured: Standard $5,000 USD (or local equivalent) family floater plan.
5. Hospital Network: Access to a network of cashless-claim hospitals (TPA details provided).
6. Accident Insurance (PA): Personal Accident policy covering disability or death, equal to 3x annual salary.
7. Term Life Insurance: Group Term Life policy covering death, equal to 2x annual salary.
8. Annual Health Check-up: One free, comprehensive health screening per year for all employees.
9. Retirement Plan (401k / PF): Company contribution matching 100% of employee contribution up to 4% of base salary.
10. Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Free, confidential 24/7 access to counseling (mental, financial, legal).
11. Wellness Allowance: Annual stipend of $300 for gym memberships, fitness apps, or wellness activities.
12. Home Office Setup: One-time $500 allowance for new hires to set up an ergonomic home office (chair, desk).
13. Internet & Phone Reimbursement: Monthly stipend of $50 to cover remote work connectivity.
14. Professional Development Fund: $1,000 per year for certifications, courses, or conferences.
15. Tuition Assistance: Up to $5,000 per year for approved higher education degrees (e.g., Master's).
16. Internal Training Access: Free access to all content on Pluralsight, Coursera, and O'Reilly.
17. Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP): Option to purchase company stock at a 15% discount.
18. Performance Bonus: Annual discretionary bonus based on company and individual performance.
19. Referral Bonus: Graded bonus ($1,000 - $5,000) for successfully referring new hires.
20. Meal Vouchers / Cafeteria: Subsidized meals in the office cafeteria and/or digital meal vouchers.
21. Company Transport: Free shuttle/cab services for employees working from the office (in select locations).
22. Paid Parental Leave: Extends beyond statutory requirements (e.g., 26 weeks maternity, 10 weeks paternity).
23. Adoption Assistance: Paid leave and financial assistance ($2,000) for employees adopting a child.
24. Relocation Assistance: Comprehensive package (moving, temporary housing) for employees moving for a role.
25. Employee Discounts: Corporate discounts on partner products (e.g., laptops, cars, travel).
26. Flexible Spending Account (FSA): Option to set aside pre-tax money for medical or dependent care.
27. Anniversary/Milestone Awards: Service awards for completing 5, 10, and 15 years with the company.
28. Commuter Benefits: Pre-tax benefits for public transport used for commuting to the office.
29. Company-sponsored Events: Annual team-building offsites, family days, and holiday parties.
30. Volunteer Time Off (VTO): 2 paid days per year to volunteer at a charity of choice.
