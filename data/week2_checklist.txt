WEEK2 CHECKLIST

Goal: Shift from "Setup" to "Learning & Integration."
Participation: Actively participate in all daily team stand-up meetings.
Project Immersion: Review the last 3-6 months of project documentation in Confluence.
Code/Work Review: Shadow a teammate; review their code (PR) or work (design file/report).
Manager 1:1: Come prepared to your second manager 1:1 with specific questions about team processes and goals.
Tasking: Aim to complete 1-2 small, well-defined tasks (e.g., bug fixes, documentation updates).
Team Process: Understand the team's "Definition of Done" for a task.
Team Process: Review the team's branching strategy and code review guidelines (if applicable).
Coffee Chats (Cross-functional): Schedule 1-2 coffee chats with people *outside* your immediate team (e.g., in Product, Design, or QA).
Product Knowledge: Attend a demo of the company's main product or service.
Architecture Review: (If Engineer) Review the high-level system architecture diagrams.
Design System Review: (If Designer) Review the Figma design system and component library.
Data Dashboard Review: (If Analyst) Review the primary data dashboards (e.g., in Tableau/Power BI).
Customer Persona: Understand the key customer personas the team builds for.
Competitor Review: Identify and review the top 2-3 competitors.
HR Check-in: Confirm your benefits enrollment is complete and correct in the HRMS.
Payroll Check-in: Confirm your bank details and tax information are correctly entered in the payroll system.
Tooling: Explore advanced features of the primary tools (e.g., Jira filters, Slack reminders).
Internal Groups: Join at least one non-work-related Slack channel (e.g., #pets, #hobby-coding, #gaming).
Employee Resource Groups (ERGs): Review the list of ERGs and consider joining one.
Performance Goals: Begin drafting your 90-day performance goals in the HRMS.
Ask for Feedback: Ask your manager or team "buddy" for initial feedback on your first tasks.
Learning Portal: Complete at least one technical or soft-skill course from the company's learning platform.
Team Calendar: Understand the team's recurring meetings (e.g., Sprint Planning, Retrospective).
Hardware: Confirm your requested home office equipment (monitor, etc.) has an estimated delivery date.
Office Visit: (If hybrid) Complete your first in-office "anchor day" successfully.
Travel Policy: Review the travel_policy.txt if your role involves travel.
Communication Rules: Review the communication_rules.txt for etiquette.
Submit Timesheet: Complete and submit your timesheet for Week 2.
Peer "Buddy": Check in with your assigned onboarding buddy with any "silly questions" you've saved up.
